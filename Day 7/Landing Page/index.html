About FindLink

FindLink is a community-driven platform dedicated to reuniting people with their lost items. We believe that every lost item has a story and every found item has an owner waiting to be reunited.

Our mission is to create a safe, reliable, and efficient way for people to report lost items and help others find what they're looking for. Whether you've lost your keys, phone, or a precious family heirloom, our community is here to help.
